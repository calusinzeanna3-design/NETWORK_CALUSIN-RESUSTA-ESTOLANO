NEW ENHANCED TEAM WITH ORIENTED KNOWLEDGE

The New Enhanced Team With Oriented Knowledge (NETWORK) is a student-led organization committed to empowering future professionals through seminars, workshops, and collaborative activities focused on innovation and leadership.

NETWORK builds a strong academic community by promoting teamwork, enhancing technical skills, and fostering growth among BIT students.

Through its initiatives, the organization prepares members to adapt and thrive in a fast-changing technological environment.
